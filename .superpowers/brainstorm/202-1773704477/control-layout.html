How it works

  • Each player uses one thumb on the joystick (outer bottom corner) and one thumb on the fire button (inner bottom corner)
  • Joystick tracks touch offset from its center — ship moves in that direction continuously
  • Fire button tap spawns a bullet if fewer than 3 are active
  • Multi-touch handled independently per side — touches on the left half never interfere with the right
  • Controls are mirrored: Imperial joystick is bottom-left, Rebel joystick is bottom-right
