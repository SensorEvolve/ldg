Touch Control Layout

Each player controls their ship on one half of the screen. Which control scheme feels right?

⚡

A — Virtual Joystick + Fire Button

Thumb joystick in the corner for movement, big fire button on the opposite side. Classic arcade feel. Each player gets their own joystick + button in their half.

⬆

B — Drag to Move + Tap to Fire

Drag anywhere in your half to move the ship. Tap with a second finger to fire. No fixed buttons — feels like touching the ship directly. Very natural on touch screens.

C — D-Pad + Fire Button
